About

You just found a one-stop-shop. Lewter Art Solutions. Big and small brands enjoy working with our small, dynamic team of creative minds focused on integrated marketing. Do you need to deeply connect with your ideal clients through brand development, traditional & digital marketing, advertising, Facebook Ads, workshops, SEO, media/photography that turns an ROI, strategic content creation that matches your brand voice? Say the word. You name it - we can either do it or partner with our network to get you down the road. Lewter Art Solutions loves seeing our clients win their horizon. Founded in 2021, by Gage Lewter, we’ve worked with clients internationally and coast to coast in the US. We’re based in Texas and we love it here!

Now that you've gotten to know us and seen what we've built, let's work on something together. At Lewter Art Solutions, we believe in giving back to the art community. We love to put on Graphic Design Workshops. So if you are a Graphic Designer, and would like to volunteer, let us know!
